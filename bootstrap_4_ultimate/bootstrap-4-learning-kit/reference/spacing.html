Utilities: Spacing

  
{property}{sides}-{size} for all breakpoints
{property}{sides}-{breakpoint}{size} for specific breakpoints and up

Properties:
m - margin
p - padding

Sides:
t - top
r - right
b - bottom
l - left
x - left and right
y - top and bottom
[blank] - all four sides

Breakpoints:
sm - small
md - medium
lg - large
xl - extra large

1 - 0.25rem
2 - 0.5rem
